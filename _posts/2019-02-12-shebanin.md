Shebanin is actually a service based ecommerce where anyone can order a service with a schedule. The concept of Shebanin is like Sheba.xyz. A digital wallet also integrated here. People can deposit through Walletmix gateway to their Shebanin wallet and can order service, recharge their mobile by that wallet balance. Vendor (actually service provider) can register and take a order to complete that. After successfully completed that order, vendor will given about 80% of total amount by Shebanin.com. Partner can join here as a referrar. They can order for a offline person and can earn money. We are also developing 3 mobile apps for Shebanin.com. Those are Customer app (Ecommerce), Partner app and Vendor app.
